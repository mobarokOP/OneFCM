FAQ

Questions, answered

Is the beta really free?

Yes. While OneFCM Cloud is in beta, every feature is free — unlimited apps, generous device and notification allowances, analytics, the lot. We're using the beta to harden the platform, and your feedback is the price of admission.

What happens after the beta?

We'll introduce paid cloud plans with clear notice well before anything changes. Beta users will keep a meaningful free tier, and if a paid plan isn't for you, you can export your data and move to self-hosting at any time — the software itself stays free under MIT.

Can I migrate between self-hosted and cloud?

In both directions. The cloud runs the same open-source OpenFCM codebase, so your apps, App IDs and API integrations work identically. Point your SDK's baseUrl at the new server, re-upload your Firebase service account, and devices re-register automatically on the next app launch.
